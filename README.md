# SCP-575
 EXILED plugin for SCP:SL that randomly causes blackouts and spawns SCP-575 throughout the round. Loosely based on the version by joker-119.
 
 By default, a blackout has a 40% chance of occuring every 5-10 minutes. During a blackout, tesla gates and large doors are disabled, and players will take damage from SCP-575 every 5 seconds unless they have an active flashlight equipped, or they're holding a gun with an active flashlight on it.
